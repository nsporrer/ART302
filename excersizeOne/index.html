Disc Golf Discs

Overview:

There are three broad categories of disc golf discs. Each of these disc types have certain advantages and disadvantages.

Here are the three main types:

  • Putters
  • Mid-Ranges
  • Drivers

Disc Flight Paths:

Description:

As you can see in the image, putters are designed to fly straight, but not super far. Mid-ranges are designed to go farther than putters but with a little sacrifice to accuracy. Drivers can be thrown the longest, but they will hardly ever go straight, making them the hardest disc to learn how to throw.

Within each of the three broad categories of discs there are more sub categories. Some discs go left on their own, some right, and some relatively straight. Click here to learn more about further classifying of discs.
